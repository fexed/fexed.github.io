Quria, Ocrilia...

What's this about?

Quria and Ocrilia are, respectively, a planet and its moon orbiting Ontalia, a star in my fictional universe.
The star, the planets, the cultures and the events are all born from my imagination and the imagination of my party, during the DnD campaigns we are playing.

This page is a summary of the Ocrilia universe, meant to give an idea of the setting to players and DMs without giving too much away.
