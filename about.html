About

Federico Matteoni

I was born on August 9th 1996 in Lucca, Italy.
I attended the E. Fermi high school in Lucca, and on October 8th 2021 I graduated in computer science at the University of Pisa, where I currently attend the Master's Degree in Artificial Intelligence.
On June 2022 I begun working as a software engineer at Garmin Italy Technologies srl.

Throughout my life I was always fascinated by science: astronomy and computers have really captured my heart and mind, but every aspect of science still has a strong pull on me.
Every field that requires imagination, inventiveness, ingenuity and reasoning captures my attention. I like to delve into stuff, both in an abstract and concrete sense: I like exploring, experimenting, discovering how things work and try to replicate, use or modify them.

In my free time, among the other things, I like reading, taking photos, playing tabletop RPGs and videogames, and working on personal projects.
